Introduction

If the aesthetics are mapped to variables, ggplot2 automatically creates legends wherever applicable. You may want to modify the appearance of legends. In this module, we will learn to modify the legends when fill is mapped to categorical variables.

Agenda

Modify legend when fill is mapped to variables. Specifically

  • title
  • breaks
  • limits
  • labels
  • values

Plot

Introduction

ggplot(mtcars) +
  geom_point(aes(disp, mpg, fill = factor(cyl)), shape = 22)

ggplot(mtcars) +
  geom_point(aes(disp, mpg, fill = factor(cyl)), shape = 22) +
